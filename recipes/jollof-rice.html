Jollof Rice Recipe

Jollof rice is an african dish known for its addictive tendencies and delicious flavour, it combines sauce with white rice garnished with healthy vegitables and sometimes a meat of choice

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Bell Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Meat of your choice
  • Corn and other Veggies

Steps

  1. Wash The Rice
  2. Parboil The Rice
  3. Prepare your sauce
  4. Mix and wait till rice becomes soft
  5. Garnish with Veggies
